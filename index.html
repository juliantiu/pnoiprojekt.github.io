Why am I in this class?

I am in this class, because I realized pretty late that I was a visual learner. Additionally, not only was I a visual learner, but also a kinesthetic one; so I thrive in visually interactive environments. I mean, you guys have no idea how much time I spent drawing Dijkstra's algorithm step by step, over and over again, just to make it stick to my head. With the advent of technologies such as interactive applications, websites, and even virtual reality, I hope to work on products and applications in the future that will help the young visual learners succeed in their environment. Also, because my GPA needs a little help...
